About

Team Name: Krishi Yoddha

Krishi Yoddha is an AI-based farmer query support system designed to assist farmers with actionable insights. The platform aims to provide disease detection, crop and fertilizer recommendations, multilingual voice support, and key market/weather information. This will help improve productivity, reduce losses, and make informed decisions.

The initiative focuses on accessibility, simplicity, and regional language support so that every farmer can benefit from modern technology.

Our Mission

Empower every farmer with timely, localized, and AI-assisted guidance for better outcomes.

Our Vision

Build a trusted digital companion for Indian agriculture with inclusive technology.
